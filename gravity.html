Gravity
Introduction
Gravity is a University project undertaken in my second year. The task was to produce a micro design document, art style guide and a prototype game with test iterations.

I designed a simple mobile game around a previous mechanic I had developed a few weeks prior. The aim of the game is to navigate your ship on it's last boost through space, using black holes to slingshot yourself to the safety of your mothership.

The demo includes 5 levels, and 2 locked sectors, as a prototype for this unit. The player will earn a star by completing the level, and is able to collect 2 more within the level by colliding the ship with them. Stars are used to unlock the next sector (See video).

Features
Gravity mechanics, animated UI, developed sprites, game saving, and sound effects.
